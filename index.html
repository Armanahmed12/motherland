Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Fostering a culture of creativity and technology-driven solutions to drive economic growth and global competitiveness.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally responsible practices to ensure long-term prosperity for both people and the planet.

Infrastructure

Developing modern and resilient infrastructure that supports connectivity, commerce, and quality of life.

Empowerment

Equipping citizens with education, skills, and opportunities to actively participate in building a progressive nation.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

“Bangladesh stands at the threshold of a transformative era. Our goal is to build a nation driven by innovation, guided by sustainability, and powered by the collective strength of its people. Together, we can create an economy that leaves no one behind — where technology, education, and empowerment work hand in hand to secure a brighter, more equitable future for all.”

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
